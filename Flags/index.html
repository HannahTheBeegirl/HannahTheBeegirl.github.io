    a xenogender in which one feels an extremely strong connection to cats or other felines

  • plushgender
    a xenogender that relates to plushies and soft toys and feels soft, comforting, and almost nostalgic

    attraction to all genders, however, gender usually still plays a role in one's attraction

    unable to distinguish the difference between romantic and platonic attraction ... due to one's status as neurodivergent

  • polyamorous
    the capability or desire to be in a relationship with more than one individual at once
